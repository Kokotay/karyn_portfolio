About me

About me

My name is Karyn, and I am a graphic design student with a unique background in psychology and English. I have always been fascinated by the way words and images can be used to convey complex ideas and emotions, and I am excited to be pursuing my passion for design as a way to communicate and connect with others.
As a new designer, I am constantly exploring new techniques and styles to create work that is both visually striking and emotionally resonant. I believe that design has the power to inspire, inform, and transform the world around us, and I am committed to using my skills to create meaningful experiences for others.
One of my key strengths as a designer is my background in psychology. Through my studies, I have developed a deep understanding of human behavior and motivation, which I bring to every project I work on. I am always thinking about the user experience and how I can create designs that are both aesthetically pleasing and intuitive to use.
In addition to my passion for design, I am also a writer and avid reader. I believe that storytelling is at the heart of great design, and I love to explore how words and images can work together to create a powerful narrative. Whether I am creating a brand identity, a website, or a social media campaign, I always strive to tell a story that will engage and connect with my audience.
As an empathetic person, I am deeply committed to creating designs that are inclusive, accessible, and respectful of diverse perspectives. I believe that design has the power to bring people together and create positive change in the world, and I am excited to be part of a community of designers who share this vision.
Overall, I am a curious, creative, and empathetic person who is passionate about design and its ability to make a difference in the world. I am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to learn, grow, and create, and I can't wait to see where my journey as a designer takes me next.
